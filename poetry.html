1 / 6 / 2023: Free Response to Poetry Quote
Nameless are the thoughts that once were
Unknown, Uninspected, Hidden
With poetry, their name we refer
Known, inspected, Heard

They speak so loud
Discererning hopes and fears
Potential high as a cloud
With poetry, their meaning clears

Yours and mine thought survives
As we continue our daily lives
With poetry, we carve away
A structure out of thought-out clay
1 / 17 / 2023: Spielberg's Perspective on (1) Childhood, (2) Success, (3) Challenges
Speilberg’s childhood contained lots of challenges and struggles like many others childhoods. One’s childhood leads to their adulthood and this journey describes who we are and nobody knows who we are until we tell them this story. Our childhood and what we do with it determines our future. Spielberg used the stories of his childhood to achieve success. His movies each included tidbits from his past as he struggled with describing his childhood to the world. Throughout his life, he experienced many challenges, from conflicts in his youth to interpreting his life into films.

1 / 31 / 2023: Two Names, Two Worlds
Each name, a world
One name, one world
Two names, Two worlds
Each world, a name

One world, a broken one
Societal rubble
Issues and conflict won
A world in trouble

Second world, a complete one
Societal utopia
Issues and Conflict of none
A resting cornucopia

Two worlds, each given a name
A name fitting of one’s despair
A name fitting of two’s success.
A name fitting of their identity.
